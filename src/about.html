About Alpenglow Plumbing & Mechanical

Local, owner-operated plumbing and mechanical services in Summit County, Colorado. With over 20 years of experience, we're dedicated to serving mountain homes with reliable, honest work.

Our Mission

At Alpenglow Plumbing & Mechanical, we believe in doing good work for good people. As a local, owner-operated business with over 20 years of combined experience in Summit County, we understand the unique challenges of mountain living. Our mission is simple: provide honest, reliable plumbing and mechanical services that our neighbors can trust.

We're not a corporate chain. We're your neighbors, working in the communities where we live. Every job matters, every customer deserves respect, and every problem deserves a proper solution.

Trusted by homeowners and local property managers throughout Summit County.

The Alpenglow Story

Alpenglow Plumbing & Mechanical was founded on the principle that plumbing and mechanical work should be done right, done cleanly, and done with integrity. After decades of working in the trade, we saw too many homeowners getting poor service from big shops focused on volume over quality.

We started Alpenglow to be different. To take the time to understand each home's unique needs. To explain problems clearly and solutions honestly. To build relationships with our customers, not just complete transactions.

The name "Alpenglow" reflects what we value—that beautiful moment when mountain peaks catch the first or last light of day. It's a reminder that quality work, done well, is its own reward. We're here to serve Summit County for the long haul, building a reputation one job at a time.

How We Work

What you can expect when you work with us:

Clear Communication

We explain problems in plain language, provide honest estimates, and keep you informed throughout the work. No surprises, no upsells, no pressure.

Quality Craftsmanship

Every job is done right the first time. We take pride in clean work, proper installation, and systems that will last. Our work reflects our name.

Respect for Your Home

We treat your home like our own. Clean work areas, protection of your property, and leaving things better than we found them.

Local Knowledge

Over 20 years working in Summit County means we understand mountain homes, cold weather systems, seasonal properties, and the challenges unique to high-altitude living.

Fair Pricing

Honest work at fair prices. We charge what the job is worth, not what we think we can get. No hidden fees, no unnecessary parts, no inflated labor.

Standing Behind Our Work

If something isn't right, we'll make it right. Period. Your satisfaction and trust are more important than our bottom line.
